I'm Leaving Discord
Yes, Seriously

March 1st, 2022

So, you've heard what I've been saying; I'm leaving Discord


Don't worry tho, you will still see me online in other places, like Revolt. This decision is personal, and because of many reasons.


Hopefully here I can clarify what some of those are.

  • Privacy

    • I have a lot of privacy concerns with Discord. Even with 2FA someone was able to get into my account, because Discord prefers tokens over any form of authorisation.
    This means that if anyone has your Discord token, they can login without the app requesting any form of verification.

  • Their new ToS/Community Guidelines rule-out (March 28th)

    • Their new ToS/CC dictates they can use media however they like.
    As someone who has written a dodgy ToS/CC personally, I understand what they mean by what they are saying.

  • Pay to Use

    • While Discord is still free to use, many features are paid, while on an app like Revolt there are the same features and more, completely free!

  • Bugs and Reporting them

    • If memory serves me correct, Discord pulled away all of their main "Guilds" where you could freely report a bug. This simply makes reporting them harder, and I see no point in making an end-user experience any harder.


With all of that said, I hope not to bother anyone with leaving the service. My guilds will still be available, with their icons, but a lot of media I uploaded myself has been removed.


Unfortunately, this does mean I will be leaving Top.gg also
